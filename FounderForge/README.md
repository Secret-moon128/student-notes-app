# 🚀 FounderForge

Turn your startup idea into a complete business blueprint.

FounderForge helps entrepreneurs, students, and innovators transform raw startup ideas into structured startup plans, MVP roadmaps, and investor-ready pitches.

---

## 🌟 Features

### Startup Blueprint Generation

Generate:

- Problem Statement
- Target Audience
- Revenue Model
- Unique Value Proposition
- Go-To-Market Strategy

---

### MVP Generator

Automatically create:

- Core Features
- Future Features
- Development Priorities

---

### SWOT Analysis

Analyze:

- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Opportunities
- Threats

---

### Competitor Analysis

Identify:

- Existing Solutions
- Competitive Advantages
- Market Opportunities

---

### Investor Pitch Generator

Generate a concise startup pitch suitable for:

- Investors
- Incubators
- Hackathons
- Startup Competitions

---

### Startup Success Score

Get a calculated startup viability score based on:

- Market Potential
- Differentiation
- Monetization Strategy
- Scalability
